1. Executive Summary

ADL Coin (ADL) is a specialized liquidity protocol built on the Ethereum blockchain, specifically tailored for cross-border institutional capital management.

2. The Problem: Trapped Capital

"Enterprises often lose up to 90% of their operational liquidity due to rigid local capital controls."

In many emerging markets, strict foreign exchange laws prevent businesses from efficiently repatriating profits. This leads to massive idle capital that cannot be reinvested, effectively trapping wealth within a single jurisdiction.

3. The ADL Solution

ADL creates a "Liquidity Bridge" by connecting local currency pools with global digital assets. Our protocol allows businesses to:

  • Convert local currency into ADL-backed liquidity certificates.
  • Transfer value instantly via Ethereum's secure infrastructure.
  • Settle in any target jurisdiction through our network of global partners, bypassing traditional banking bottlenecks.
